This could be the cutest project that I've worked on so far! It's pretty much a dragon Tamagotchi (I might be aging myself by admitting I had one)
We created a virtual dragon that you could feed, walk, put to sleep- all without having to leave the comfort of your chair. Of course, I almost hate ASCII art now.
I created mine using a loop with embedded if statements so I could get user input on what to do with the dragon. In the second stage of the project we grouped up and my group decided we'd give our dragon a life span. After playing a certain number of times the dragon flies the nest.
