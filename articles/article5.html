The one with the Farmers Markets

September 9th 2016

Our predecessors tried to warn us. I distinctly remember a Game of Thrones themed meme that I chuckled at thinking, "it can't possibly be that bad!"

I was definitely wrong. Michelle Nathan from a previous cohort put it best:

"FarMar is the first multi-day project we’ve tried that dealt with multiple classes, multiple CSV files and multiple tables. FarMar is short for Farmers Market and shorthand for “this is gonna suck for you”. Let me tell you, it lived up to its promise…it sucked." --Michelle
It was a week-long struggle, but success!!!

FarMar was supposed to be the culmination of everything we learned in the previous 5 weeks of school. And, yes. It did indeed suck.
